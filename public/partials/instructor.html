Why I Chose Yoga?

I began practicing yoga simply to stay fit, but over time it became much more than a fitness routine. It evolved into a powerful stress reliever, bringing me peace of mind and a sense of inner balance. As I continued my journey, I noticed positive changes not only in my physical health but also in my overall well-being. Yoga helped me feel energetic, youthful, and vibrant. Today, it keeps me healthy, happy, and feeling young at heart.
